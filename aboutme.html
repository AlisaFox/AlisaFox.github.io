Hey there! I'm Alisa, a second year student in Mcgill University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Cognitive Science. I love exploring different areas of the digital world and learning how to apply them to the real world. I've often been described as inventive, rational, and determined, with a practical approach to life.

Outside of school, I am a very sporty person. I've done dancing, taekwondo, karate, swimming, yoga, and weight-lifting. I've been swimming since I was 8, so due to all that experience, I started working a swim instructor with both kids and adults once I was 16.

My hobbies are ever changing. I've gone from knitting and making friendship bracelets to animating with Blender and playing with Arduino. Back in high school, I took drama, arts, and music (instead of sticking with one like most people do) due to my inquistive nature. However, I pledge that, I will always love cats, good food, coffee, and sleep.
